Biography

CPA Mark Okeyo has vast experience in financial management and reporting. He previously worked for various companies and NGOs in managerial roles. He was the Africa Finance & Capacity Building Manager for Edmund Rice Foundation Africa, overseeing operations in Kenya, Tanzania, South Sudan, Uganda, Zambia, and South Africa between 2019 and 2022. He later joined politics and was elected as the MCA for West Asembo, Rarieda Sub-County in Siaya County.

He holds a Masters Degree in Business Administration (Accounting Option) from the University of Nairobi and a Bachelor of Commerce degree (Finance Option). He is also a qualified accountant registered with ICPAK. Mark prides himself on transformative leadership and sustainable development.
